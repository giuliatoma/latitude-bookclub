Book Club

A literary book club · English-speaking · Berlin

I'm looking for a café that feels like the right home for a small, serious book club. Once a month, a group of curious adults — 5 to 10 people — meets to discuss a novel from a different country or culture. No special setup. Everyone pays for their own drinks.

The idea is simple: each session we read a book set in a different part of the world. We discuss over coffee for about two hours on a weekday evening, starting around 6:30pm.
